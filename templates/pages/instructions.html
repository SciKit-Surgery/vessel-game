Controls
Use the mouse to move your camera.
Use the arrow keys (or the W,A,S and D keys) to move your cutting instrument.
Cut by holding the left mouse button, dragging the cursor over a vessel, then releasing!
You can press the spacebar to pause at any time.

How to play
Cut the vessel which has two black ends.
Don't be fooled by the others! You'll get a time penalty!
The size of the camera will vary in each level.
Try to solve each level as fast as you can, with the least instrument movement.
